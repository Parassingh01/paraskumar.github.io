TIC TAC GAME

Tic-Tac-Toe is a classic two-player game played on a 3x3 grid. One player uses "X" and the other uses "O." The players take turns placing their respective symbols in empty grid spaces, with the goal of forming a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line of three of their own symbols. The first player to achieve this wins the game. If all the grid spaces are filled without a winner, the game ends in a draw. It is a simple yet strategic game often used to teach basic concepts of logic and planning.
